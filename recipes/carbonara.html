Carbonara

Description

This recipe creates a deliciously homebaked carbonara!

Taking simple ingredients like cheese, pasta and chicken; we combine them effectively using traditional techniques making this wonderful dish.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Chicken
  • More cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Boil water, add pasta
  2. Heat oil in pan, add chicken
  3. Mix cheese and egg in bowl
  4. Add sauce into pan with chicken and cooked pasta, toss briefly and serve
  5. Enjoy :)
