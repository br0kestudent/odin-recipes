Pasta Bake

Description

This recipe creates a deliciously home cooked steak!

Taking simple ingredients like beef, potato and tomatoes; we combine them effectively using traditional techniques making this wonderful dish.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Potato
  • Tomatoes
  • Honey & Mustard sauce

Steps

  1. Heat oil in pan, add beef
  2. Add tomatoes into pan
  3. Cook chips in oven
  4. Fry steak and tomatoes until cooked
  5. Serve with sauce and enjoy :)
