Pasta Bake

Description

This recipe creates a deliciously homebaked pasta bake!

Taking simple ingredients like pasta, bacon and cheese; we combine them effectively using traditional techniques making this wonderful dish.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Heat oil in pan, add bacon
  2. Boil pasta
  3. Add pasta and bacon into dish
  4. Cover in sauce and cheese
  5. Enjoy :)
