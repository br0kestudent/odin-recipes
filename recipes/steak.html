Steak

Description

This recipe creates a deliciously home cooked steak!

Taking simple ingredients like beef, potatoes and tomatoes; we combine them effectively using traditional techniques making this wonderful dish.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Potatoes
  • Tomatoes
  • Honey & Mustard

Steps

  1. Fry steak and tomatoes in the pan
  2. Cook chips in oven
  3. Combine together on plate
  4. Add sauce and chips
  5. Enjoy :)
